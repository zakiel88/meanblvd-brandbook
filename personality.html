The Story Behind

Our Story

The name MEAN represents four characteristics — Maverick, Elegant, Artisanal, Nobby — that form the essence of the brand's personality. BLVD (Boulevard) signifies a straight path — a clear, direct route from Vietnamese fashion excellence to the global stage.

Logo Inspiration — "Chim Lạc"

Our visual identity draws from the legendary Chim Lạc — the mystic bird of Vietnam found on the ancient Đông Sơn bronze drums, artifacts representing thousands of years of Vietnamese civilization. The geometric symbol represents letters M-E-A-N interwoven in a diamond form, opening a path from Vietnam to the four directions of the world.
