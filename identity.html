Design Origins

Logo Inspiration

Our logo draws from the legendary Chim Lạc — the mystic bird of Vietnam — found on the ancient Đông Sơn bronze drums. These artifacts represent thousands of years of Vietnamese civilization and artistry.

Symbolism

The geometric symbol represents the letters M-E-A-N interwoven in a balanced diamond form. It symbolizes a "boulevard" path opening from Vietnam to the four directions of the world — reflecting both craftsmanship and cultural identity through modern interpretation of ancient motifs.
