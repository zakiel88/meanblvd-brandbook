Guidelines

Do's & Don'ts

Do's

  • Be polite and professional in all communications
  • Use formal, precise vocabulary
  • Use warm greetings: "Welcome back!"
  • Write: "Warm greetings from MEAN BLVD"
  • Maintain enthusiastic yet respectful tone
  • Use full word forms consistently
  • Incorporate key words: Authentic, Curated, Exclusive, Excellence

Don'ts

  • Don't use contractions (write "do not" instead of "don't")
  • Avoid slang and colloquialisms
  • Never write: "Hi baby" or "Hi love"
  • Don't be overly casual or informal
  • Avoid generic or cold language
  • Never use abbreviations in formal content
  • Do not mix languages within sentences
