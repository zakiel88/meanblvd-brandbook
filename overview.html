01 — Brand Overview

Brand Overview

Understanding MEAN BLVD's mission, vision, and the global market we serve.

Mission

To build a "straight boulevard" that leads Vietnam's top local fashion brands to the global market — pioneering the promotion of "made-in-Vietnam" quality internationally.

MEAN BLVD (MEAN BOULEVARD) là một platform bán lẻ, phân phối chính hãng các sản phẩm từ các thương hiệu thời trang thiết kế nữ cao cấp của Việt Nam tới thị trường quốc tế.

Short-term Vision — 3 Years

Establish & Solidify

Become the premier retail platform for Vietnamese local fashion. Strengthen distribution channels, establish MEAN BLVD as the leading platform, and solidify a global logistics network.

Long-term Vision — 3+ Years

Expand & Lead

Act as a gateway for Asian local fashion — Thailand, South Korea, and beyond — connecting unique regional styles with global consumers and becoming the primary gateway for Asian fashion worldwide.
